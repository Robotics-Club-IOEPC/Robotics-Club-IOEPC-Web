YANTRA 8.O

Robotics Association of Nepal (RAN) celebrate festival of science, technology and entrepreneurship festival “Yantra”. Yantra was initiated by Robotics Association of Nepal in 2012 as a national robotics competition, similarly this year(2019) RAN organized a international event as YANTRA 8.O with several competitions likes manual akhada, automatic akhada, art and tech exhibition etc. we participate in automatic akhada and secured first position.
